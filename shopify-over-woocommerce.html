Why choose Shopify over woocommerce



Let’s first understand what is Shopify? And what services does it offer?

It's an online tool that allows you to create an eCommerce shop.

You do not need to hire a programmer, and you will be guided by Shopify’s interface and support if you get stuck on some of those measures. And, there's a massive community of Shopify users and experts out there willing to help (if that is not enough).

With Shopify, you can sell all sorts of products such as digital downloads, drop shipped products, and services. You can incorporate your own Shopify shop, all thanks to their Point-Of-Sale devices if you have a brick-and-mortar store.

If you are brand new to eCommerce or simply need a simple solution, Shopify is undoubtedly a reliable option. It allows you to get up and run your business fast.

Shopify vs. WooCommerce:

  • Both programs are outstanding e-commerce options that offer similar features and functionality.
  • Your choice will likely depend on differences in pricing versions between WooCommerce and Shopify.
  • Shopify is excellent for all those brand new to eCommerce or those who need a turnkey solution.
  • WooCommerce is ideal for those who want full control over their eCommerce store and also want the power of WordPress

The information we collect could only be used for the following purposes:

How do we protect our users’ information?

The information acquired from our visitors cannot be distributed, sold or otherwise shared with third parties without the visitor’s explicit consent, excluding the cases where specific product must be delivered for the visitor by the affiliating company, or the government authorities are requesting it. We may use the user’s contact email address to send an occasional promotional message, website or content updates, or a direct sales offer. The user holds an option to seize receiving such emails by following the ‘unsubscribe’ procedure steps which can be initiated from a link included in all the promotional emails we send. We invested a huge amount of resources in keeping all users’ information as secure as possible. All of our internal website links are protected by SSL. We deem all of our visitors’ information as confidential and thus we protect it with the latest encryption methods.

Do we share visitors’ information to outside parties?

We do not trade, exchange or sell our users’ identifiable information to outside parties, excluding the cases where we have to give contact information to our partnered merchants in order to deliver or otherwise process user’s specific order. All of our partnered merchants would be abiding the same privacy agreements as listed in this page for keeping all users’ information confidential. However, all non-identifiable information may be passed out to third parties for advertising, marketing or other reasons.
